Interactive World

Project Description

In ICS 162, I was tasked with creating a living interactible world by models from scratch. I needed to create a player model from scratch as well as create objects the player can interact with.

First off, the player model. I created my player model based off the character, Mr. Meeseeks from Rick and Morty, as I wanted to have a cartoonish looking character and translate into a 3D enviroment.

I then created various objects the player can interact with. The items being a soccer ball and spinning obsctacles to mess around with the physics in the Unity Game Engine.

In additon, I created a gate and goal the player can inteact with their keyboards. This was meant to experiment with both player and physical interactions in the game.
