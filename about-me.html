About Me

Everything About Me

My name is Alexis Padilla, I am a student at UCI. I am a Computer Game Science major who specializes in Human Computer Interactions. I love to play video games. So much that I dedicated my life to create games. I focus on the UI aspects of video games as I believe communicating with the player is as important as the gameplay and graphics of said games. It is important that the player can understand what is going on in the game.

That said, I have also worked on other projects that relates to UI Design and other programs not related to UI. With these projects, I hope they allow you to understand how studious I can be.
